# Electronic Fund Transfer Error Resolution (staff summary)

This is a paraphrased operational summary for training — not a reproduction of regulatory text. Always consult current official sources and Legal before acting.

## Customer notice window
Consumers generally should report electronic fund transfer errors within **60 days** of the statement that first reflected the error.

## Bank investigation timing
For many consumer EFT errors, provisional credit may be required within **10 business days** if the investigation is not completed, with final determination typically within **45 days** (longer for certain new-account or foreign-initiated transfers).

## Staff talking points
- Acknowledge the claim and collect required facts
- Do not promise a specific refund amount before investigation completes
- Explain that provisional credit may be reversed if the claim is denied
- Document all customer contacts in the case file
