# Service Response Standards

## Tone
Be clear, calm, and precise. Prefer verified figures from core systems over estimates.

## What agents must not say
- Do not guarantee loan approval or investment returns
- Do not provide tax, legal, or personalized investment advice
- Do not disclose whether a SAR was filed
- Do not share another customer's information

## Co-pilot usage
When using FinSight Service Co-Pilot suggestions, staff must review citations and edit for accuracy before sending. The live agent remains accountable for the final message.

## Escalation
Escalate to a licensed banker for complex credit products and to the Fraud desk for suspected account takeover.
