# Customer Identification Program (CIP) SOP

## Required identity elements (new retail accounts)
Collect and verify at account opening:
1. Full legal name
2. Date of birth
3. Residential address (no P.O. box as sole address for CIP)
4. Identification number (SSN/ITIN for U.S. persons; passport + country for non-U.S.)

## Documentary verification
Accept unexpired government photo ID (driver license, state ID, passport). Record ID type, number, issuer, and expiration in the CIP log.

## Non-documentary verification
When documentary evidence is incomplete, use a combination of: consumer reporting agency match, deposit account reference, or digital identity vendor. Escalate to BSA Officer if verification fails.

## Beneficial ownership (legal entity customers)
For legal entity customers, identify beneficial owners with **25%+ equity** and one control person. Retain Certification of Beneficial Owner form.

## Record retention
Retain CIP records for **five years** after account closure.
