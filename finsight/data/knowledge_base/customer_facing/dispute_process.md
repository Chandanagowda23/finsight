# Disputing a Transaction

## When to dispute
You may dispute a charge that is unauthorized, incorrect in amount, or for goods/services not received as described.

## What we need
- Transaction date and amount
- Merchant name
- Reason for the dispute
- Any supporting documentation (receipts, cancellation emails)

## Timeline
Provisional credit, when applicable, may be issued within **10 business days** of a completed dispute claim for qualifying debit card errors. Card network chargebacks follow network rules and can take **45–90 days**.

## Important
Filing a dispute does not guarantee a refund. False claims may result in account restrictions. FinSight digital assistants can **draft** a dispute ticket, but a human specialist must approve filing.
