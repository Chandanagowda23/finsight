# FinSight Savings Rate Sheet (effective 2025-06-01)

## Standard Savings
- APY: **3.50%** on balances up to $25,000
- APY: **3.75%** on balances above $25,000
- Minimum opening deposit: $25
- Monthly fee: $0 when e-statements are enabled; otherwise $4

## High-Yield Savings (relationship)
Available when you also hold Everyday Checking with qualifying direct deposit.
- APY: **4.25%** on balances up to $100,000
- APY: **4.00%** on balances above $100,000
- Limit of 6 convenient withdrawals per month (Reg D style internal limit); excess withdrawal fee **$10**

## Rate changes
Deposit rates are variable and may change at any time. The APY shown assumes monthly compounding. Fees may reduce earnings.
