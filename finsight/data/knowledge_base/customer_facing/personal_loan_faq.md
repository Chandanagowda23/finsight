# Personal Loan FAQ

## What amounts can I request?
Personal loans typically range from **$2,000 to $35,000** with terms of 24–60 months. Exact offers depend on underwriting.

## What are typical rates?
Illustrative APR ranges are **9.99%–19.99%** for highly qualified borrowers. Your rate is determined after a formal credit review — FinSight assistants cannot approve or deny credit.

## Are there origination fees?
A one-time origination fee of **1%–3%** of the loan amount may apply and is disclosed in your loan agreement before acceptance.

## Can I prepay?
Yes. There is **no prepayment penalty** on FinSight personal loans originated after January 1, 2024.

## Is an eligibility pre-check a credit decision?
No. Any eligibility estimate provided by FinSight digital channels is **informational only** and is not an offer, approval, or commitment to lend.
