# FinSight CashBack Visa

## APR
- Purchase APR: **18.99%–24.99%** variable based on creditworthiness and Prime Rate
- Penalty APR: **29.99%** may apply if you are late more than 60 days
- Cash advance APR: **26.99%** with a 3% cash advance fee ($10 minimum)

## Fees
- Annual fee: **$0**
- Late payment fee: up to **$40**
- Foreign transaction fee: **3%** of the U.S. dollar amount
- Returned payment fee: up to **$29**

## Rewards
Earn **1.5% cash back** on everyday purchases and **3% cash back** on grocery and transit categories, capped at $300 cash back per calendar year for the 3% category. Cash back is not earned on cash advances, balance transfers, or fees.

## Liability for unauthorized use
Under applicable consumer protection rules, your liability for unauthorized card use may be limited when you report promptly. Contact FinSight Card Services at 1-800-555-0199 immediately if your card is lost or stolen.
