# FinSight Everyday Checking

## Monthly maintenance fee
The Everyday Checking account has a monthly maintenance fee of **$12**. The fee is waived when you meet any one of the following each statement cycle:
- Direct deposit totaling $500 or more
- Combined average daily balance of $1,500 across Everyday Checking and FinSight Savings
- Student status with valid .edu verification (waiver for up to 5 years from account opening)

## Overdraft
Overdraft protection courtesy pay may cover ATM and one-time debit transactions up to $100 when enabled. A **$30** overdraft fee applies per item, maximum 3 fees per day. Extended overdraft fee of **$15** applies if the account remains overdrawn for 5 consecutive business days.

## Interest
Everyday Checking is a non-interest-bearing transactional account.

## Account closure
Accounts with a zero balance may be closed after 60 days of inactivity. Closing an overdrawn account does not forgive the negative balance; repayment remains due.
